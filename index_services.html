Business Strategy

Comparing small local service networks to large international platforms:

Local networks offer personalization, trust, flexibility, and community support. They cater to specific community needs and often operate with lower overheads and greater agility compared to larger networks.

Large networks provide extensive reach and standardized services but may lack the nuanced understanding and personal touch of local networks. The choice depends on the intended scope, audience, and market needs.

Technical Stack

Overview of the technology used in our service platforms:

Frontend: React, Next.js, or Svelte depending on the project type.

Backend: Supabase for database management and authentication, with Postgres as the database. Edge functions are used to connect Stripe for payment processing, directly integrated with the company account. AI customer support bots are implemented using LangChain and LangGraphs, running on a server with Flowise, providing API services for each website.
